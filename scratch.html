The obvious answer is Novak Djokovic

Statistics Overview

  1. Facts and numbers
    • He has won 24 Grand Slam titles, the most in history.
    • He has held the world No. 1 ranking for a record 428 weeks (Over 8 years as the highest ranked player), the most in history.
    • He has a winning record against all of his main rivals, including Roger Federer (27-23) and Rafael Nadal (31-29).
    • He has won 40 Masters tournaments, the most in history.
    • He has won all nine ATP Masters 1000 events at least twice, nobody else has even done that once.
    • He has had the best individual tennis season of all time (2015) and has a record 16,950 ranking points held at one time, the most in history.
    • He is the only player to have won the ATP tour finals, Olympic Gold, and every Grand Slam.

  2. Qualitative Descriptions
    • He is known for his incredible mental toughness and ability to perform under pressure.
    • He has a unique playing style that combines power, precision, and athleticism.
    • He has refined his game to perfect every detail, leaving no exploitable weaknesses.
    • He managed all of these feats in the time of two other GOAT contenders (Federer and Nadal), managing to break through and overcome them despite all odds.
    • He has had amazing success across decades, and he is still playing. His olympic gold medal was just this last year in 2024, at the age of 37.
